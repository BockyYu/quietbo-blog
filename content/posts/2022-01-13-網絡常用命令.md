## <span class="ez-toc-section" id="curl%E5%91%BD%E4%BB%A4"></span>curl命令<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span> {.wp-block-heading}



請求web服務的

<ul class="wp-block-list">
  <li>
    -v :顯示請求詳細信息
  </li>
</ul>

<pre class="wp-block-code"><code class="">curl quietbo.com  -v</code></pre>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
  <li>
    -X :指定请求方式(GET or POST)
  </li>
</ul>

<pre class="wp-block-code"><code class="">curl -X Get URL
curl -X POST URL</code></pre>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
  <li>
    -H :指定頭部類型&Cookie
  </li>
</ul>

<pre class="wp-block-code"><code class="">curl -H "Content-Type:application/json"
curl -H "Cookie:username=XXX" URL</code></pre>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
  <li>
    -d :使用POST向server發送數據，下方可以省略-X POST
  </li>
</ul>

<pre class="wp-block-code"><code class="">curl -H "Content-Type:application/json" -d '{"name":"bocky","date":"2022/1/13"}' URL -v</code></pre>
